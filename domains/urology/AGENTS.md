# Urology Domain - DDD Documentation

## Overview

This domain applies Domain-Driven Design (DDD) to urology systems, covering clinical assessment, surgical management, oncologic urology, stone disease, incontinence management, and male reproductive health.

## Bounded Contexts

1. Clinical Assessment Context - urological examination, urodynamics, cystoscopy, imaging interpretation
2. Surgical Management Context - robotic surgery, laparoscopic procedures, endourology, reconstructive urology
3. Oncologic Urology Context - prostate cancer, bladder cancer, kidney cancer, staging, surveillance
4. Male Reproductive Health Context - infertility evaluation, vasectomy, erectile dysfunction, testosterone
5. Stone Disease Context - nephrolithiasis evaluation, ESWL, ureteroscopy, PCNL, metabolic workup
6. Incontinence Management Context - stress/urge incontinence, pelvic floor therapy, surgical interventions
