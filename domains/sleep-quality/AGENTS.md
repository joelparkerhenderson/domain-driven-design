# Sleep Quality Domain - DDD Documentation

## Overview

This domain applies Domain-Driven Design (DDD) to sleep quality management systems, covering sleep assessment, sleep disorder management, sleep environment optimization, behavioral interventions, device integration, and outcomes tracking.

## Bounded Contexts

1. **Sleep Assessment Context** - Sleep diaries, questionnaires (PSQI, ESS), actigraphy, polysomnography.
2. **Sleep Disorders Context** - Insomnia, sleep apnea, restless legs syndrome, narcolepsy, parasomnias.
3. **Sleep Environment Context** - Bedroom optimization, light/noise/temperature control, mattress/pillow selection.
4. **Behavioral Interventions Context** - CBT-I, sleep restriction therapy, stimulus control, relaxation techniques.
5. **Device Integration Context** - Wearables, smart mattresses, CPAP data, ambient sensors.
6. **Outcomes Tracking Context** - Sleep efficiency trends, quality scores, daytime functioning, satisfaction.
