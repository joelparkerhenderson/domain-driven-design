# Dermatology Domain - DDD

This domain applies Domain-Driven Design to dermatology systems: clinical assessment, lesion management, procedure management, cosmetic services, pathology integration, and treatment outcomes.

## Bounded Contexts

1. Clinical Assessment Context - skin examination, dermoscopy, lesion documentation, classification systems
2. Lesion Management Context - lesion tracking, biopsy management, excision planning, follow-up scheduling
3. Procedure Management Context - Mohs surgery, cryotherapy, laser therapy, phototherapy protocols
4. Cosmetic Services Context - aesthetic consultations, injectable treatments, skin rejuvenation
5. Pathology Integration Context - biopsy specimen tracking, histopathology reports, staging
6. Treatment Outcomes Context - treatment effectiveness, wound healing, recurrence tracking, photo comparison
